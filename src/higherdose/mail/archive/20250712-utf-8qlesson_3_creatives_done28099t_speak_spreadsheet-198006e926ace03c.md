Hey Jourdan,
In the last email, we discussed how creatives are the new ad targeting.
And if that’s true — you need to acknowledge something fundamental: your media performance is entirely dependent on creative. Your designers and UGC creators are closer to the revenue function than they’ve ever been.
And you might be surprised to know that the creators on your team want detailed feedback on what’s working and what isn’t.
But they need to receive that feedback in a language they can understand.
What they aren’t going to understand: your media buying spreadsheets.
You live in Ads Manager and Excel, they live in Adobe and Figma. It’s classic right brain vs left brain, creative vs analytical.
That’s why you need a translator. A way to connect the data to the content.
Part of this is a process problem, but part of it is a people problem too.
