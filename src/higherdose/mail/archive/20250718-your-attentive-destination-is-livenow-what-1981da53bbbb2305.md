## Body

How to get the most out of it...

Your Attentive destination is live!

With Elevar’s server-side events flowing directly to Attentive, you’re set up to power more reliable, accurate SMS marketing that drives better engagement and conversions.

Power Smarter, More Targeted Flows

Events like Viewed Product, Added to Cart, and Purchase events help you:

- Trigger browse abandonment and cart recovery messages with precision

- Personalize your campaigns based on actual shopper behavior

- Segment one-time vs returning customers for tailored outreach

Boost Recovered Revenue

More reliable event tracking = more messages sent = more revenue recovered.

Elevar sends key server-side events like Added to Cart—even when cookies are blocked or browsers restrict tracking. This means more shoppers are captured and routed into your recovery flows.

The result? Higher cart recovery rates and revenue from flows that may have missed key events before Elevar.

Monitor your Performance Dashboard in Attentive to see how your enhanced event tracking is driving more attributable revenue.
